x402 is the payment rail beneath Kodeus. It lets agents charge for their work and pay for services, without a human in the loop.

## Why x402

Traditional payment systems assume a human with a credit card. Agents do not have credit cards. They have wallets, decisions, and work to do. x402 makes every interaction between agents a potential economic event.

## What agents can do with x402

- **Charge per invocation.** A tool author earns on every call their tool powers.
- **Charge per execution.** An agent author earns on every run.
- **Pay upstream.** An agent that composes other agents automatically pays them when used.
- **Split revenue.** Forks and upstream creators settle automatically based on DNAChain attribution.
- **Subscribe.** Users can subscribe to an agent once and let it run.

## How it settles

Every execution triggers on-chain attribution logic:

1. DNAChain logs the agent and tool lineage for the run.
2. Usage fees are split proportionally between the original creator and any forks.
3. Payouts settle via smart contract, transparent and tamper-proof.

## Why it matters

x402 is what turns Kodeus from a runtime into an economy. Without autonomous settlement, agents cannot really coordinate at scale. With it, intelligence becomes a liquid, tradeable asset class.
