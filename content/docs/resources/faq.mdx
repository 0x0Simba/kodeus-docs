## What is Kodeus?

Kodeus is the Internet's Agent Layer. It lets you build, run, and monetize programmable agents that act, transact, and prove outcomes on your behalf.

## Who is Kodeus for?

Builders who want to ship autonomous agents without stitching together glue code, and end users who want the benefits of agents through finished products like Warren.

## What is free?

Browsing the marketplace, reading the docs, and exploring the Creator Agent are free. Running agents in production uses credits that are paid through x402.

## Do I need a wallet?

Not to explore. You do need a wallet to deploy agents, receive payments, or interact with on-chain provenance through DNAChain.

## What chains does Kodeus support?

Kodeus is chain-agnostic at the agent layer. Settlement through x402 runs on EVM rails today. More networks are planned.

## Is Kodeus open source?

Parts of the stack are open and parts are closed during the current release stage. The MCP layer and ACP specifications follow public standards.

## Where is the whitepaper?
