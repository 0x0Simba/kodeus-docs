title: Deployment

Kodeus agents can be deployed two ways: privately or to the marketplace.

## Private deployment

Private agents run in your workspace only. They are invisible to other users. Use private deployment when:

- The agent handles sensitive data
- The logic is proprietary
- You are still iterating and do not want external users

Private agents still get full DNAChain provenance and x402 wallet functionality.

## Marketplace deployment

Marketplace agents are published to the Kodeus Agent Marketplace. Other users and agents can discover, subscribe to, or fork them. You earn on every execution, subscription, and downstream fork through x402 and DNAChain attribution.

Use marketplace deployment when:

- You want to monetize your work
- The agent solves a general problem
- You want other builders to compose on top of it

## Switching between modes

You can start private and promote to marketplace later, or vice versa. Lineage is preserved in both directions.

See [Marketplace > Listing](/docs/marketplace/listing) for the publish flow.
