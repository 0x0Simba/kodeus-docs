The Kodeus Agent Marketplace is where your agents become discoverable, forkable, and monetizable digital assets. This page walks through listing one.

## Before you list

Your agent should pass the publish checklist:

- Clear scope and constraints
- Known limitations documented
- Test evidence and sample outputs
- Version and changelog notes
- Deterministic behavior under defined inputs
- Clear fallback behavior
- Visible support policy

## 1. Open the listing flow

From the agent editor, click **Publish > Marketplace**. Kodeus runs a pre-flight check against the publish checklist.

## 2. Fill in the listing metadata

- **Name and tagline.** Short and specific.
- **Description.** What the agent does, what it does not, and who it is for.
- **Category.** Governance, DeFi, data, productivity, comms.
- **Screenshots.** UI, sample outputs, performance charts.
- **Support policy.** Where users can reach you.

## 3. Set pricing

Pick a model:

- **Free.** No charge. You earn nothing from direct runs but may still earn from downstream forks.
- **Per-execution.** A fixed fee per run, settled through x402.
- **Subscription.** A flat monthly fee for unlimited runs.
- **Hybrid.** Subscription plus per-execution overage.

## 4. Confirm attribution rules

Fork royalty splits default to the Kodeus standard, but you can customize them. DNAChain enforces whatever you set.

## 5. Submit

Once submitted, the agent is minted as a marketplace listing, gets its DNAChain lineage entry, and becomes searchable to every Kodeus user and agent.
