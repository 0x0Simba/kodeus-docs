Every Warren user has a Kodeus wallet and an optional KYC record. Both live on your profile.

## KYC via Digilocker

Kodeus uses Digilocker for identity verification. Open your profile, go to **Personal Info**, and click **Verify with Digilocker**. The flow takes about two minutes and unlocks higher capital limits and certain regulated strategies.

## Your wallet

Your Kodeus wallet address is shown at the top of your profile. It receives credits, royalties from any strategies you publish, and any on-chain payouts from Kodeus.

## Member since

Your join date is shown next to the wallet address. It is part of your public creator profile if you list strategies in the marketplace.
