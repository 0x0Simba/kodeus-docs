---
title: Modes
description: Live, Paper, and Demo modes. When to use each and how to switch.
---

Warren runs in one of three modes. You pick a mode during onboarding and can switch any time from your profile.

## Live Trading

Real funds on a connected exchange. Every order Warren places is executed against your exchange account. Use this once you have validated a strategy in Paper mode and are comfortable with the guardrails.

## Paper Trading

Simulated capital, real market data. Warren behaves exactly as it would in Live mode but no orders are sent to the exchange. Use this to:

- Test new strategies before risking capital
- Validate guardrail settings
- Practice the workflow

## Demo

A read-only walkthrough of the UI with seeded data. No exchange connection required. Use this to explore Warren before you decide to onboard.

## Switching modes

Open your profile, go to **Preferences**, and pick the mode. Switching to Paper or Demo pauses any Live bots. Switching back to Live restores them in the same state.
