---
title: Capital
description: Set and adjust the volume Warren manages on your behalf.
---

The capital allocation tells Warren how much of your exchange balance it can deploy across strategies. It is a hard ceiling, not a target.

![Volume](/images/warren/onboarding-volume.png)

## Setting your initial allocation

During onboarding, Warren asks for a starting volume. Pick an amount you are comfortable losing in the worst case. A common starting point is 5 to 10 percent of your total exchange balance.

## Adjusting later

Open your profile and update the volume from **Preferences**. Warren rebalances on the next refresh:

- Increasing the volume frees Warren to scale existing bots or deploy new strategies.
- Decreasing the volume tells Warren to wind down positions until total exposure fits the new ceiling.

## Per-strategy capital

The total volume is split across active bots. Each bot has its own allocation that you set when deploying. The sum of bot allocations cannot exceed the total volume.
