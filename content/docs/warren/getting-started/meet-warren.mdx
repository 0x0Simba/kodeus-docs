Warren is the AI fund manager built into Kodeus Trading Bay. You talk to Warren in plain English and it does three things:

1. **Analyzes markets** in real time across major pairs
2. **Discovers and creates strategies** matched to your risk profile
3. **Runs bots** that execute strategies on your connected exchange

Warren is not a chatbot. Not a signal group. Not a copy-trade service. It is an autonomous execution agent that operates on your behalf, within limits you define.

![Meet Warren](/images/warren/meet-warren.png)

## Three guarantees

- No manual trading required. Warren handles execution.
- Your funds stay in your exchange. API keys only, no withdrawals.
- You stay in control. Set guardrails, pause anytime, override any recommendation.

## What Warren is not

- Not a custodian. Funds live on Binance, Kraken, OKX, or Bybit.
- Not a black box. Every trade is logged in your portfolio with full attribution.
- Not financial advice. Always review strategies before deploying.
