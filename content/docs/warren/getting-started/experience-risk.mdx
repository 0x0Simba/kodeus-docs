---
title: Experience and Risk
description: Reference for the four experience levels and three risk profiles Warren uses to tailor recommendations.
---

Warren tailors strategy recommendations based on two inputs you set during onboarding: your experience level and your risk profile. You can change either at any time from your profile.

## Experience levels

- **Beginner**. New to crypto trading. Warren defaults to simple, well-documented strategies with the tightest guardrails.
- **Novice**. Familiar with the basics. Warren expands the strategy set and introduces risk metrics in plain language.
- **Experienced**. Active trader. Warren surfaces a wider mix including breakout, scalping, and grid strategies, with full metrics shown by default.
- **Seasoned**. Professional. Warren unlocks every strategy type, advanced filters, and the AlphaCraft Autopilot Listener for fine-grained control.

## Risk profiles

- **Conservative**. Capital preservation first. Smaller position sizes, lower turnover, tighter drawdown limits. Bias toward mean reversion and trend following on majors.
- **Balanced**. Diversified mix across strategy types. Controlled volatility. Moderate position sizing. Suitable for most users.
- **Aggressive**. Momentum and breakout heavy. Larger positions, higher turnover, wider drawdown limits. Expect bigger swings in both directions.

## Changing your settings

Open your profile, go to **Preferences**, and adjust experience or risk. Warren reweights its recommendations on the next refresh. Existing bots keep running until you pause or replace them.
