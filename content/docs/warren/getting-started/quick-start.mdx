title: Quick Start
description: Onboard Warren in seven steps.
---

## 1. Open Trading Bay

From the Kodeus home screen, select **Trading Bay** in the top nav. Warren greets new users with a prompt asking what they need.

![Trading Bay](/images/warren/home-warren-prompt.png)

## 2. Pick your experience level

Warren tailors strategy recommendations to the user's trading background.

Options: **Beginner**, **Novice**, **Experienced**, **Seasoned**.

## 3. Choose a risk profile

![Risk profile](/images/warren/onboarding-risk.png)

- **Conservative**: capital preservation, smaller positions, slower turnover
- **Balanced**: diversified mix, controlled volatility
- **Aggressive**: momentum-heavy, larger positions, higher volatility

## 4. Set the volume to manage

![Volume](/images/warren/onboarding-volume.png)

Pick a starting capital allocation. This can be changed at any time from the profile.

## 5. Review suggested strategies

![Suggestions](/images/warren/onboarding-strategies.png)

Warren selects starter strategies based on the inputs above, matched to the current market regime. Users choose between Live Trading, Paper Trading, or Demo mode at this step.

## 6. Choose a mode

- **Live Trading**: real funds on a connected exchange
- **Paper Trading**: simulated capital, useful for validating a strategy before committing real money
- **Demo**: explore the UI without connecting anything

## 7. Connect your exchange

Supported: Binance, Kraken, OKX, Bybit. Kodeus requests API access only. Users retain full custody of funds, trading securely with their own keys.
