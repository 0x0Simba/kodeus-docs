## P/L

Profit and loss is reported in two flavors.

### Realized P/L

The profit or loss from closed positions. This is locked in. Realized P/L only changes when a bot closes a trade.

### Unrealized P/L

The profit or loss on currently open positions, marked to the live market price. Unrealized P/L moves continuously and becomes realized when the position closes.

The portfolio dashboard sums both. Today's P/L equals realized today plus unrealized on positions still open at end of day.

## Win Rate

Win rate is the percentage of closed trades that were profitable, calculated as winning trades divided by total closed trades. Open positions do not count.

## What win rate does not tell you

A high win rate can hide a fragile strategy. A bot with 90 percent win rate but a single catastrophic loss can still be net negative. Always read win rate alongside max drawdown and average win-to-loss ratio.
