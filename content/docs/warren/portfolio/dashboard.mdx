The portfolio dashboard is your home base in Trading Bay. It shows everything Warren is managing across every connected exchange.

![Portfolio](/images/warren/portfolio.png)

## Top metrics

- **Total Volume**. The capital ceiling you set for Warren across all exchanges.
- **Today's P/L**. Realized plus unrealized profit and loss for the current day.
- **Bots Running**. Count of active bots.
- **Win Rate**. Percentage of closed trades that were profitable across all bots.

## P/L splits

Below the top metrics, P/L is broken down three ways:

- **Total P/L**. Lifetime across all bots and exchanges.
- **Trading Bay P/L**. Profits attributable to Warren-managed strategies.
- **Exchange P/L**. Profits from positions you opened manually outside Warren.

## Connected exchanges

A list of every exchange Warren is connected to, with current balance, open positions, and connection health. Click any exchange to drill into its specific holdings.

## Capital allocation

A breakdown of where your volume is currently sitting: In Trading Bay, Idle, or Open Positions. See [Capital Allocation](/docs/warren/portfolio/capital-allocation) for definitions.

## Activity panels

Two panels run down the right side of the dashboard: a Recent Trades feed showing every fill across your bots, and a Live Market Feed showing relevant price movements for the assets you trade.
