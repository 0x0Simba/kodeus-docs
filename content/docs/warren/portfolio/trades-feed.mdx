title: Trades Feed and Live Market
description: The recent trades panel and the live market sidebar.
---

Two activity panels run alongside the dashboard.

## Recent Trades

A chronological feed of every fill across all your bots. Each row shows:

- Time
- Bot name
- Asset and side (buy or sell)
- Price and size
- P/L if the trade closed a position

Click any trade to jump to the bot it came from. Use the filter to narrow by exchange or asset.

## Live Market Feed

A sidebar that streams price movements for the assets your bots care about. Pairs are pulled automatically from your active strategies. The feed shows last price, 24h change, and a sparkline.

The Live Market Feed is purely informational. It is not a trading interface. Use it to understand context for what your bots are doing.
