The fastest way to find a strategy is to ask Warren for one. Open the chat in Trading Bay and describe what you want in plain English. Warren searches the marketplace, applies your risk profile, and returns the closest matches.

![Chat workflow](/images/warren/chat-workflow.png)

## Example prompts

- `Find me a low-risk BTC strategy that performed well this month`
- `Show grid bots for ETH that work in sideways markets`
- `What is the best performing momentum strategy on SOL right now?`
- `I want a strategy with at least 60 percent win rate and under 20 percent drawdown`

## How discovery works

Warren reads your prompt, calls the strategy index, filters by your risk profile and capital ceiling, and ranks the results by recent performance. The chat shows the workflow visualization at the top so you can see which tools were called.

## From chat to deployment

Each result is a strategy card. Click any card to open the full strategy page where you can review the backtest, risk metrics, and deployment options. Warren stays in the chat ready for follow-up questions like `compare these two` or `show me something less aggressive`.
