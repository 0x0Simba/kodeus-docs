The fastest way to find a strategy is to ask Warren directly. Open the chat in Trading Bay and describe what is needed in plain English. Warren searches the marketplace, applies the user's risk profile, factors in the current market regime, and returns the closest matches.

![Chat workflow](/images/warren/chat-workflow.png)

## Example prompts

- `Find me a low-risk BTC strategy that performed well this month`
- `Show grid bots for ETH that work in sideways markets`
- `What is the best performing momentum strategy on SOL right now?`
- `I want a strategy with at least 60 percent win rate and under 20 percent drawdown`

## How discovery works

Warren reads the prompt, calls the strategy index, filters by risk profile and capital ceiling, and ranks results by recent performance. Recommendations are regime-aware: when market conditions shift (e.g. from trending to range-bound), Warren adjusts which strategies surface and nudges users toward better-suited options.

The chat displays a workflow visualization at the top showing which tools were called during the search.

## From chat to deployment

Each result is a strategy card. Click any card to open the full strategy page to review the backtest, risk metrics, and deployment options. Warren stays in the chat for follow-up questions like `compare these two` or `show me something less aggressive`.
