Warren is also a read interface for your portfolio. Ask it about any active bot, time window, or strategy and it pulls live numbers from your dashboard.

## Example prompts

- `How is my BTC momentum bot doing this week?`
- `Which of my bots is underperforming?`
- `Show me today's P/L across all exchanges`
- `Compare my two grid bots side by side`
- `Why did my SOL bot stop trading yesterday?`

## What Warren returns

For each query Warren shows:

- The relevant numbers: ROI, win rate, trade count, drawdown
- A short interpretation in plain English
- A recommended action when something looks off

## Acting on the review

If Warren flags an underperforming bot it will suggest pausing, reallocating, or replacing it. You can act directly from the chat by replying with `pause it`, `cut allocation in half`, or `replace with a balanced strategy`. Warren confirms before any change reaches the exchange.

## Regime shift alerts

Warren also pushes proactive nudges when market regime changes. These appear in the chat with context and recommended adjustments. Read the context, review your active bots, and act.
