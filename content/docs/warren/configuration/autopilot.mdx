The AlphaCraft Autopilot Listener is Warren's execution layer. It controls how much autonomy Warren has when reacting to market conditions and bot performance.

## Autonomy levels

- **Guided**. Warren proposes every change. You approve before anything reaches the exchange. Best for new users.
- **Semi-auto**. Warren executes routine adjustments (rebalancing, paused-bot resumes) on its own. Material changes (deploying new strategies, increasing total exposure) still require your approval.
- **Full auto**. Warren acts on its own across the full guardrail surface. You only see notifications after the fact.

## When to move up

Start with Guided. Move to Semi-auto after your first profitable week. Move to Full auto only after you trust Warren through at least one market regime change.

## Controls

The Autopilot tab in your profile exposes:

- Autonomy level
- Poll interval (how often Warren checks the market)
- Auto-pause on regime shift
- Auto-restart after emergency stop (off by default)

## Hard limit

Autopilot never overrides your guardrails. Max drawdown, daily loss limit, and per-position size are absolute. Warren stops, regardless of autonomy level, when any guardrail trips.
