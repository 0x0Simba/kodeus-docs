---
title: Credits
description: What credits are, what they unlock, and how to top up.
---

Credits are the unit of account inside Trading Bay for non-trading actions. They cover the things that cost compute or third-party data on Kodeus's side.

## What credits cover

- **Warren chat queries**. Each prompt that calls market data or strategy creation tools.
- **Backtests**. Running a strategy against historical data on demand.
- **Paper trading**. Simulated execution at live market data rates.
- **Premium strategy fees**. Some Community strategies are priced in credits.

## What credits do not cover

- Live trading itself. Warren does not charge per trade.
- Connecting exchanges, viewing your portfolio, or running already-deployed bots.

## Topping up

Open your profile and go to **Credits**. Buy a top-up pack with card or crypto. Credits do not expire.

## Free monthly allowance

Every account gets a free monthly credit allowance that resets on the first of each month. The allowance covers casual usage. Heavy users will need to top up.
