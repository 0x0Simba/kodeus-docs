---
title: Notifications
description: Configure how and when Warren reaches you.
---

Warren sends notifications for trades, regime shifts, guardrail trips, and chat replies. You control the channels and the noise floor.

## Channels

- **In-app**. Always on.
- **Email**. Configurable per category.
- **Telegram**. Optional, link your account from the Notifications tab.

## Categories

- **Trade fills**. Every fill, or only summaries.
- **Bot warnings**. Underperformance, paused bots, errors.
- **Regime shifts**. Warren nudges when the market changes.
- **Guardrail trips**. Always on. Cannot be disabled.
- **Chat replies**. When Warren responds to a long-running query.

Set each category to Off, Summary, or All. The defaults favor signal over noise.
