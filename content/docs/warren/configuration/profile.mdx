Your profile is the home for everything personal: identity, preferences, exchanges, credits, and Autopilot. Open it from the avatar in the top right of Trading Bay.

## Personal Info

The first tab. It contains:

- **Display name**. Shown on your creator profile if you publish strategies.
- **Email**. Used for notifications and account recovery.
- **Wallet address**. Your Kodeus wallet, prefilled.
- **Member since**. Your join date.
- **KYC status**. Verified or unverified. Click **Verify with Digilocker** to start the flow. See [KYC and Wallet](/docs/warren/connecting/kyc-wallet).

## Other tabs

- **Preferences**. Risk profile, experience level, mode, total volume.
- **Exchanges**. Connected exchanges and API key management.
- **Credits**. Credit balance and usage.
- **Autopilot**. AlphaCraft Autopilot Listener configuration.
