## Does Warren take custody of my funds?

No. Funds stay in your exchange account. Warren only has API trading permissions, never withdrawal access.

## Which exchanges are supported?

Binance, Kraken, OKX, and Bybit. You can connect more than one and Warren will track them in a single portfolio.

## Are there fees for using Warren?

Warren itself is part of Trading Bay. Some Community strategies are priced by their creators (free, per-deployment, performance share, or monthly subscription). Compute-heavy actions like backtests and chat queries cost credits.

## Can I try Warren without real money?

Yes. Switch to Paper Trading mode to use simulated capital with live market data, or Demo mode to walk through the UI without connecting an exchange.

## How do I stop a bot?

Open the dashboard, find the bot in the active-bots panel, and click Stop. All open positions are closed and the bot shuts down. You can also pause to halt new orders without closing positions.

## Is KYC required?

Basic usage does not require KYC. Higher capital limits and certain regulated strategies do. Verify with Digilocker from your profile.

## Can Warren withdraw money from my exchange?

No. Kodeus only requests trading permissions when you connect an exchange. Never enable withdrawal permissions on API keys used with any third-party platform.

## What if a strategy starts losing?

Your guardrails (max drawdown, daily loss limit, per-position size) act as circuit breakers. If losses hit your limit, Warren stops the bot. Warren also flags underperforming bots with a red warning so you can pause them before the limit trips.

## Which trading pairs are supported?

Whatever your connected exchange supports. Warren operates across spot and perpetual markets on majors and most listed alts. Strategy availability depends on the asset and the creator.

## How do I get help?

Ask Warren directly in the Trading Bay chat for product questions. For account or billing issues, reach out via the Kodeus Discord.
