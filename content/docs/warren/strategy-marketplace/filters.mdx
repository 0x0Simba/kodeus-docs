The filter panel lets you narrow the marketplace by structured criteria. Filters stack: each one you add intersects with the others.

## Risk Level

- **Low**. Capital preservation, tight stops, low drawdown ceiling.
- **Medium**. Balanced exposure, moderate drawdown.
- **High**. Aggressive sizing, wide drawdown, higher upside.

## Strategy Type

- **Momentum**. Buys strength, sells weakness.
- **Breakout**. Enters on range break, exits on reversion.
- **Scalping**. High-frequency, small-profit trades.
- **Grid**. Lays buy and sell orders at fixed intervals across a range.
- **Mean Reversion**. Fades extremes, expects price to return to average.
- **Trend Following**. Rides confirmed trends with trailing stops.

## Market

Filter by asset or pair: BTC, ETH, SOL, majors only, alts, perps, spot.

## Timeframe

The candle interval the strategy operates on: 1m, 5m, 15m, 1h, 4h, 1d.

## Performance

Filter by historical metrics: ROI range, win rate, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown.

## Capital Range

The recommended capital band for the strategy. Strategies outside your set volume are filtered out by default.

## Execution Style

- **Discrete**. Fires occasional, deliberate trades.
- **Continuous**. Always in the market in some form.
- **Scheduled**. Runs only at fixed times or on specific events.

## Complexity

- **Simple**. Single signal, easy to understand.
- **Composite**. Multiple signals combined.
- **Advanced**. Multi-leg, multi-timeframe, or ML-driven.

## Saving filter sets

Once you have a useful combination, save it as a preset from the filter panel. Presets are private to your account and one click to reapply.
