title: Strategy Cards
description: How to read a strategy card in the marketplace.
---

Every strategy in the marketplace appears as a card. The card is the summary view. Click into it for the full backtest, risk metrics, and deployment options.

## What each field means

- **Name**. The strategy title set by the creator.
- **ROI**. Return on investment over the displayed window. Hover to switch between 24h, 7d, 30d, and all-time.
- **Win Rate**. Percentage of closed trades that were profitable. A high win rate alone is not enough. Always check drawdown.
- **Risk Badge**. Low, Medium, or High. Set by the creator and validated against historical drawdown.
- **Source Badge**. Either **Kodeus** (official) or **Community** (creator-published). See [Kodeus vs Community](/docs/warren/strategy-marketplace/kodeus-vs-community).
- **Market**. The asset or pair the strategy trades.
- **Timeframe**. The candle interval the strategy operates on.

## Reading the card responsibly

ROI and win rate are headline numbers. They are not the full picture. Before deploying any strategy, open the detail page and check Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, and the backtest across different market regimes.
