The Strategy Marketplace hosts 450+ strategies curated by Kodeus and published by the community. Open it from the Trading Bay nav.

![Strategy marketplace](/images/warren/strategy-marketplace.png)

## Sections

The marketplace is organized into three top-level sections.

### Recommended Strategies

Warren's picks. Curated based on your risk profile, current market regime, and verified performance data. This is the fastest way to find a strategy that fits.

### Best Performing Today

Top strategies ranked by real-time performance over the last 24 hours. Use this to spot what is working right now, but check the longer-term metrics before deploying.

### Relevant to You

Personalized based on your capital ceiling, connected exchanges, and trading history. Strategies you cannot run on your current setup are filtered out.

## Market context banner

At the top of the marketplace, Warren displays a real-time market assessment. When the banner message changes, pay attention. Warren is telling you the regime has shifted and your active strategies may need adjustment.

## Searching and filtering

Use the search bar for keyword matches across strategy names, tags, and descriptions. Use the filter panel for structured queries across risk level, strategy type, market, timeframe, performance, capital range, execution style, and complexity. See [Filters](/docs/warren/strategy-marketplace/filters) for the full list.
