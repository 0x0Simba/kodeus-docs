The Strategy Marketplace hosts 450+ strategies curated by Kodeus and published by community creators. Open it from the Trading Bay nav.

![Strategy marketplace](/images/warren/strategy-marketplace.png)

## Sections

The marketplace is organized into three top-level sections.

### Recommended Strategies

Warren's picks. Curated based on the user's risk profile, current market regime, and verified performance data. This is the fastest path to a fitting strategy.

### Best Performing Today

Top strategies ranked by real-time performance over the last 24 hours. Useful for spotting what is working right now, but always check the longer-term metrics before deploying.

### Relevant to You

Personalized based on capital ceiling, connected exchanges, and trading history. Strategies that cannot run on the current setup are filtered out.

## Leaderboard

![Leaderboard](/images/warren/leaderboard.png)

The Top Traders leaderboard ranks the best-performing strategy creators and traders on the platform. It tracks total P/L, win rate, and strategy count. A separate strategies ranking table shows which strategies are delivering the highest returns over configurable time windows.

The leaderboard drives the creator economy: strategy creators earn from real usage when other users deploy their strategies. Execution-linked monetization means creators are incentivized to build strategies that actually perform, not just attract clicks.

## Market context banner

At the top of the marketplace, Warren displays a real-time market assessment. When the banner message changes, it signals a regime shift. Active strategies may need adjustment.

## Searching and filtering

Use the search bar for keyword matches across strategy names, tags, and descriptions. Use the filter panel for structured queries across risk level, strategy type, market, timeframe, performance, capital range, execution style, and complexity. See [Filters](/docs/warren/strategy-marketplace/filters) for the full list.
