---
title: Kodeus vs Community
description: The difference between official Kodeus strategies and community-published ones.
---

Every strategy card in the marketplace carries a source badge. There are two:

## Kodeus

Official strategies built and maintained by the Kodeus team. They are:

- Backtested across multiple market regimes
- Reviewed by the Kodeus risk team
- Updated when market conditions shift
- Free to deploy

Use Kodeus strategies when you want a known-good baseline.

## Community

Strategies built and published by independent creators. They are:

- Backtested by the creator
- Validated through paper trading before publication
- Rated by trader reviews and live performance
- Priced by the creator. Free, per-deployment, performance share, or monthly subscription

Use Community strategies when you want variety, niche markets, or specialist logic that the core Kodeus set does not cover.

## How to tell the difference

The badge sits in the top corner of every strategy card. The same information appears on the strategy detail page next to the creator name. If a strategy is Community, the creator profile and their other strategies are one click away.
