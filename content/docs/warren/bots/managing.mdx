---
title: Managing Bots
description: Pause, stop, reallocate, and delete bots from the active-bots panel.
---

The active-bots panel on the dashboard is your control surface for every running, paused, and stopped bot.

## Pause

Pauses execution. Open positions stay open, no new orders are placed. Use this when you want to temporarily halt a bot without closing positions, for example during high-impact news.

## Stop

Closes all open positions and shuts down the bot. Use this when you are done with a strategy or when Warren flags it as underperforming.

## Reallocate

Adjusts the capital assigned to a bot without restarting it. Increase to scale up a winner. Decrease to take risk off the table. Reallocation respects your total volume ceiling.

## Delete

Removes the bot from the dashboard. Available only after a bot has been stopped. Trade history is retained in your portfolio for accounting.

## Bulk actions

The active-bots panel supports multi-select. Pause or stop multiple bots at once during a regime shift or when reacting to a Warren nudge.

## Warren's underperformance line

Warren draws a horizontal line on the active-bots panel separating performing and underperforming bots. If a bot drops below the line, Warren shows a red warning. Consider pausing or stopping the bot to avoid further losses.
