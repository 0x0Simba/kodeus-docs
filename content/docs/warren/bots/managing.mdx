---
title: Managing Bots
description: Pause, stop, reallocate, and delete bots from the active-bots panel.
---

The active-bots panel on the dashboard is the control surface for every running, paused, and stopped bot. It shows the performance table with ROI, status, and recent trades for each bot.

![Bots management](/images/warren/bots-management.png)

## Pause

Pauses execution. Open positions stay open, no new orders are placed. Use this to temporarily halt a bot without closing positions, for example during high-impact news.

## Stop

Closes all open positions and shuts down the bot. Use this when done with a strategy or when Warren flags it as underperforming.

## Reallocate

Adjusts the capital assigned to a bot without restarting it. Increase to scale up a winner. Decrease to take risk off the table. Reallocation respects the total volume ceiling.

## Stop-loss and take-profit

Warren actively manages SL/TP levels on every open position. When market conditions change, Warren adjusts these levels and reallocates capital across bots to optimize yield. Users define the outer bounds; Warren operates within them.

## Delete

Removes the bot from the dashboard. Available only after a bot has been stopped. Trade history is retained in the portfolio for accounting.

## Bulk actions

The active-bots panel supports multi-select. Pause or stop multiple bots at once during a regime shift or when reacting to a Warren nudge.

## Warren's underperformance line

Warren draws a horizontal line on the active-bots panel separating performing and underperforming bots. If a bot drops below the line, Warren shows a red warning. Pausing or stopping the bot avoids further losses.
