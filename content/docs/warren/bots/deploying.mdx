---
title: Deploying a Bot
description: Turn a strategy into a running bot in four steps.
---

A bot is a deployed instance of a strategy. You can deploy any marketplace strategy or any strategy you built in chat.

![Dashboard](/images/warren/dashboard.png)

## Steps

1. **Pick a strategy**. Open the strategy detail page from the marketplace or from a chat result.
2. **Click Deploy**. The green Deploy button is at the top right of the strategy page.
3. **Name the bot**. Pick a name you will recognize on the dashboard. Defaults to the strategy name.
4. **Set guardrails and capital**. Allocate capital, set max drawdown, daily loss limit, and per-position size. These are hard limits Warren will never exceed.
5. **Start**. The bot enters the running state and shows up on your dashboard.

## Start small

On your first deployment, allocate 5 to 10 percent of your total volume. You can always scale up later. There is no benefit to deploying at full size before you have seen the strategy run through real market conditions.

## After deployment

Warren begins monitoring the bot immediately. The dashboard shows ROI, win rate, trade count, and running time in real time. You can pause, stop, or reallocate at any time.
