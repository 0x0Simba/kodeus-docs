---
title: Deploying a Bot
description: Turn a strategy into a running bot in five steps.
---

A bot is a deployed instance of a strategy. Any marketplace strategy or any strategy built in chat can be deployed.

![Dashboard](/images/warren/dashboard.png)

## Steps

1. **Pick a strategy.** Open the strategy detail page from the marketplace or from a chat result.
2. **Click Deploy.** The green Deploy button is at the top right of the strategy page.
3. **Name the bot.** Pick a name that is recognizable on the dashboard. Defaults to the strategy name.
4. **Set guardrails and capital.** Allocate capital, set max drawdown, daily loss limit, and per-position size. These are hard limits Warren will never exceed.
5. **Start.** The bot enters the running state and appears on the dashboard.

## Start small

On the first deployment, allocate 5 to 10 percent of total volume. Scaling up later is straightforward. There is no benefit to deploying at full size before observing the strategy through real market conditions.

## After deployment

Warren begins monitoring the bot immediately. The dashboard shows ROI, win rate, trade count, and running time in real time. Pausing, stopping, or reallocating is available at any time.

## Confirmation

Once a strategy is deployed, a confirmation screen verifies that the bot is live and running.
